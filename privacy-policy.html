Privacy Policy

Cookie Reject — Chrome Extension

Effective Date: February 27, 2026

TL;DR: Cookie Reject collects zero data. No personal information, no browsing history, no analytics, no tracking. Everything runs locally in your browser.

1. Overview

Cookie Reject ("the Extension") is a browser extension that automatically rejects cookie consent banners on websites. This Privacy Policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any data whatsoever. The Extension:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not track your browsing activity
  • Does not store any website data
  • Does not use analytics or telemetry
  • Does not send any data to external servers
  • Does not use cookies itself

3. How the Extension Works

The Extension operates entirely within your browser. It:

  • Injects CSS styles to hide known cookie consent banner elements
  • Runs JavaScript to detect and click "Reject" or "Decline" buttons on cookie banners
  • Calls Consent Management Platform (CMP) APIs to set consent to "reject"
  • Stores your enable/disable preference using chrome.storage.local (local to your device only)

All processing happens locally in your browser. No data ever leaves your device.

4. Permissions Explained

activeTab

Allows the Extension to interact with the currently active tab when you click the extension icon. Used to reload the page when toggling the extension on/off.

storage

Stores your enable/disable preference locally on your device. This data is never transmitted anywhere.

scripting

Allows the Extension to inject scripts that detect and dismiss cookie banners on web pages.

host_permissions: <all_urls>

Required so the Extension can run on any website to detect and reject cookie banners. The Extension does not read, store, or transmit any page content.

5. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party services, APIs, libraries, or analytics tools.

6. Data Storage

The only data stored is your enable/disable toggle preference, saved locally on your device via chrome.storage.local. This data:

  • Remains on your device
  • Is never synced to the cloud
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the Extension

7. Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the changes will be reflected in the Extension's listing on the Chrome Web Store and on this page. The "Effective Date" at the top will be updated accordingly.
